Ortego Dental Clinic was founded in 1964 in Barcelona, in the Galvany neighborhood (Sarrià-Sant Gervasi), by Dr. Mariano Ortego Rivero. Since then, with professionalism and dedication, our clinic has grown and established itself as a reference in dental health care.

Today, with three generations of dentists, we continue to be a family clinic whose priority isprofessional ethics, prevention, and dental health care, as well as providing personalized and close care to our patients. We want our patients to trust us and be confident that the personalized treatment plan weoffer them is undoubtedly the best option for them.
